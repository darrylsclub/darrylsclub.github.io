Q&A's

How to become staff?
Be active and engage with members to prove you are trustworthy and responsible.

What happens if I break a rule?
Depending on severity: warning, timeout, or ban. Swearing = warning, doxxing = immediate ban.

Can I appeal a ban?
Contact Mr.Black (@mr.black78) or any staff you are friends with.
